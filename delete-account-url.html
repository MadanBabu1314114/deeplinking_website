Delete Your Account for YALLA NEL3B

If you wish to delete your YALLA NEL3B account and all associated data, please follow one of these options:

  1. Open the YALLA NEL3B app and log in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account Management → Delete account.
  3. Confirm the deletion.

Alternative: Send an email to koraspace1@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject "Delete my YALLA NEL3B account". We will process your request within 7 days.

What happens when your account is deleted:

  • All personal data (profile, messages, photos, activity history, etc.) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics purposes (no personal identifiers).
  • Any data required by law (e.g., transaction or payment records, if applicable) will be retained for the legally required period (up to 7 years).
